AFHIM clothing started in 2017, born from the small dream of an individual that is currently growing more and more. From a small machine and printer used to make face masks during the pandemmic, an idea was conceived to create and venture into clothing production. Mr. Isaiah James Labay's preservance was recognized by people who also started from small dreams. In 2022, they were blessed by the Lord, all the equipment was upgraded, and they established wide connections in many places to gain mumerous customers. Currently, the have the most advanced and fastest printer in all of Mindoro, the stongest production when it comes to making full sublimated clothing, and they can supply to different places not only in Mindoro but also reach other countries. Along with the company's progress, they continue to dream of bigger and more productive services, high-quality clothing, and continue to provide services that only offer clothing but also showcase the quality of Filipino craftmanship.
